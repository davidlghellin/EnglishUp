# Present Simple - Presente Simple

## What is the Present Simple? - ¿Qué es el Presente Simple?

The Present Simple is used to talk about:
- Habits and routines (things you do regularly)
- General truths and facts
- Permanent situations
- Likes and dislikes

El Presente Simple se usa para hablar de:
- Hábitos y rutinas (cosas que haces regularmente)
- Verdades generales y hechos
- Situaciones permanentes
- Gustos y preferencias

---

## Form - Formación

### Affirmative - Afirmativo

| Subject | Verb | Example |
|:-------:|:----:|:--------|
| I | work | I **work** in a bank. |
| You | work | You **work** hard. |
| He | work**s** | He **works** in London. |
| She | work**s** | She **works** every day. |
| It | work**s** | It **works** well. |
| We | work | We **work** together. |
| They | work | They **work** at home. |

### Rule - Regla:
- **I, You, We, They** → verb (base form)
- **He, She, It** → verb + **-s** or **-es**

### Spelling Rules for Third Person (He/She/It) - Reglas Ortográficas

| Rule | Examples |
|:-----|:---------|
| Most verbs: add **-s** | work → work**s**, play → play**s**, read → read**s** |
| Verbs ending in -s, -ss, -sh, -ch, -x, -o: add **-es** | pass → pass**es**, wash → wash**es**, watch → watch**es**, go → go**es**, do → do**es** |
| Verbs ending in consonant + y: change y to **-ies** | study → stud**ies**, try → tr**ies**, fly → fl**ies** |
| Verbs ending in vowel + y: add **-s** | play → play**s**, say → say**s**, enjoy → enjoy**s** |
| **Irregular**: have → **has** | I have / He **has** |

### Examples - Ejemplos:

| Español | English |
|:-------:|:-------:|
| Yo trabajo en un hospital. | I **work** in a hospital. |
| Ella trabaja en casa. | She **works** at home. |
| Él estudia inglés. | He **studies** English. |
| Nosotros vivimos en Madrid. | We **live** in Madrid. |
| El autobús sale a las 8. | The bus **leaves** at 8. |
| Mi hermana tiene un coche. | My sister **has** a car. |

## Negative - Negativo

### Structure - Estructura:
**Subject + do/does + not + verb (base form)**

| Subject | Auxiliary | Not | Verb | Example |
|:-------:|:---------:|:---:|:----:|:--------|
| I | do | not | work | I **don't work** on Sundays. |
| You | do | not | work | You **don't work** here. |
| He | does | not | work | He **doesn't work** anymore. |
| She | does | not | work | She **doesn't work** at night. |
| It | does | not | work | It **doesn't work** properly. |
| We | do | not | work | We **don't work** Saturdays. |
| They | do | not | work | They **don't work** hard. |

### Short Forms - Formas Cortas:
- do not = **don't**
- does not = **doesn't**

### Examples - Ejemplos:

| Español | English |
|:-------:|:-------:|
| No vivo en Londres. | I **don't live** in London. |
| Ella no come carne. | She **doesn't eat** meat. |
| No tienen coche. | They **don't have** a car. |
| Él no estudia francés. | He **doesn't study** French. |
| No me gusta el café. | I **don't like** coffee. |
| No funciona. | It **doesn't work**. |

❌ **Common mistakes**:
- ❌ He don't work here.
- ✅ He **doesn't** work here.
- ❌ She doesn't works here.
- ✅ She doesn't **work** here. (verb in base form after doesn't!)

## Questions - Preguntas

### Yes/No Questions - Preguntas Sí/No

**Structure - Estructura:**
**Do/Does + subject + verb (base form)?**

| Auxiliary | Subject | Verb | Example | Answer |
|:---------:|:-------:|:----:|:--------|:-------|
| Do | you | work | **Do** you **work** here? | Yes, I do. / No, I don't. |
| Does | he | work | **Does** he **work** on Saturdays? | Yes, he does. / No, he doesn't. |
| Does | she | live | **Does** she **live** in Madrid? | Yes, she does. / No, she doesn't. |
| Do | they | like | **Do** they **like** pizza? | Yes, they do. / No, they don't. |

### Wh- Questions - Preguntas con Palabras Interrogativas

**Structure - Estructura:**
**Wh-word + do/does + subject + verb?**

| Question Word | Do/Does | Subject | Verb | Example |
|:--------------|:-------:|:-------:|:----:|:--------|
| Where | do | you | work | **Where do** you **work**? |
| What | does | she | do | **What does** she **do**? (job) |
| When | do | they | start | **When do** they **start**? |
| How | does | it | work | **How does** it **work**? |
| Why | do | you | study | **Why do** you **study** English? |
| Who | does | he | live | **Who does** he **live** with? |

### Examples - Ejemplos:

| Español | English |
|:-------:|:-------:|
| ¿Trabajas los domingos? | **Do** you **work** on Sundays? |
| ¿Dónde vive ella? | **Where does** she **live**? |
| ¿Qué haces? (trabajo) | **What do** you **do**? |
| ¿A qué hora empiezas? | **What time do** you **start**? |
| ¿Les gusta el fútbol? | **Do** they **like** football? |
| ¿Habla ella inglés? | **Does** she **speak** English? |

❌ **Common mistakes**:
- ❌ Where you work?
- ✅ Where **do** you work?
- ❌ Does he works here?
- ✅ Does he **work** here?

## When to Use - Cuándo Usar

### 1. Habits and Routines - Hábitos y Rutinas

Things you do regularly:

| Español | English |
|:-------:|:-------:|
| Trabajo de lunes a viernes. | I **work** from Monday to Friday. |
| Ella se levanta a las 7. | She **gets up** at 7am. |
| Vamos al gimnasio los martes. | We **go** to the gym on Tuesdays. |
| Él no desayuna. | He **doesn't have** breakfast. |

**Common time expressions:**
- every day / every week / every month
- always, usually, often, sometimes, rarely, never
- on Mondays / on weekends
- in the morning / in the afternoon / in the evening
- at night

### 2. General Truths and Facts - Verdades Generales y Hechos

Things that are always true:

| Español | English |
|:-------:|:-------:|
| El sol sale por el este. | The sun **rises** in the east. |
| El agua hierve a 100 grados. | Water **boils** at 100 degrees. |
| Los gatos comen pescado. | Cats **eat** fish. |
| Madrid está en España. | Madrid **is** in Spain. |

### 3. Permanent Situations - Situaciones Permanentes

Things that don't change:

| Español | English |
|:-------:|:-------:|
| Vivo en Londres. | I **live** in London. |
| Ella trabaja en un banco. | She **works** in a bank. |
| Tienen dos hijos. | They **have** two children. |
| Él habla tres idiomas. | He **speaks** three languages. |

### 4. Likes and Dislikes - Gustos y Preferencias

With verbs like: like, love, hate, prefer, want, need

| Español | English |
|:-------:|:-------:|
| Me gusta el chocolate. | I **like** chocolate. |
| Ella ama la música. | She **loves** music. |
| Odian levantarse temprano. | They **hate** getting up early. |
| Prefiero el té. | I **prefer** tea. |

## Frequency Adverbs - Adverbios de Frecuencia

These words tell us how often something happens:

| Español | English | Percentage |
|:-------:|:-------:|:----------:|
| Siempre | Always | 100% |
| Casi siempre | Usually | 80% |
| A menudo | Often | 60% |
| A veces | Sometimes | 40% |
| Rara vez | Rarely / Seldom | 20% |
| Nunca | Never | 0% |

### Position - Posición:
**Before the main verb** BUT **after the verb "to be"**

**With main verbs:**
- I **always** drink coffee.
- She **usually** goes to bed at 10.
- They **often** play football.
- We **sometimes** watch TV.
- He **rarely** eats meat.
- I **never** smoke.

**With verb "to be":**
- I **am always** tired.
- She **is usually** happy.
- They **are often** late.
- He **is sometimes** angry.
- We **are rarely** busy.
- It **is never** cold here.

❌ **Common mistake**:
- ❌ I go always to work by car.
- ✅ I **always go** to work by car.

## Complete Examples - Ejemplos Completos

### Daily Routine:
```
I wake up at 7am every day. I have a shower and get dressed. Then I have
breakfast. I usually eat toast and drink coffee. I leave home at 8am.

I work in an office in the city center. I start work at 9am and finish at
5pm. I don't work on weekends. I usually have lunch at 1pm.

After work, I often go to the gym. Then I go home and have dinner. In the
evening, I sometimes watch TV or read a book. I go to bed at 11pm.
```

### About Me:
```
My name is Maria. I live in Barcelona with my family. I work as a teacher
in a primary school. I love my job.

I speak Spanish and English. I also study French in my free time. I like
learning languages.

In my free time, I enjoy reading and cooking. I don't like watching TV
very much. On weekends, I usually meet my friends and we go to the cinema
or have dinner together.
```
