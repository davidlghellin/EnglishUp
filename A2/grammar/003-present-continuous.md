# Present Continuous - Presente Continuo

## What is the Present Continuous? - ¿Qué es el Presente Continuo?

The Present Continuous (also called Present Progressive) is used to talk about:
- Actions happening now, at this moment
- Temporary situations
- Future arrangements (plans already made)
- Annoying habits (with "always")

El Presente Continuo se usa para hablar de:
- Acciones que están ocurriendo ahora, en este momento
- Situaciones temporales
- Planes futuros (ya organizados)
- Hábitos molestos (con "always")

## Form - Formación

### Structure - Estructura:
**Subject + am/is/are + verb-ing**

| Subject | To Be | Verb-ing | Example |
|:-------:|:-----:|:--------:|:--------|
| I | am | working | I **am working** now. |
| You | are | working | You **are working** hard. |
| He | is | working | He **is working** today. |
| She | is | working | She **is working** at home. |
| It | is | working | It **is working** well. |
| We | are | working | We **are working** together. |
| They | are | working | They **are working** late. |

### Short Forms - Formas Cortas:

| Full Form | Short Form |
|:---------:|:----------:|
| I am | I'm |
| You are | You're |
| He is | He's |
| She is | She's |
| It is | It's |
| We are | We're |
| They are | They're |

## Spelling Rules for -ing - Reglas Ortográficas

| Rule | Examples |
|:-----|:---------|
| Most verbs: add **-ing** | work → work**ing**, play → play**ing**, read → read**ing** |
| Verbs ending in -e: remove -e, add **-ing** | make → mak**ing**, come → com**ing**, write → writ**ing**, have → hav**ing** |
| Verbs ending in -ie: change -ie to -y, add **-ing** | lie → ly**ing**, die → dy**ing**, tie → ty**ing** |
| Short verbs (1 syllable) ending in consonant-vowel-consonant: double last consonant + **-ing** | run → runn**ing**, swim → swimm**ing**, sit → sitt**ing**, get → gett**ing**, stop → stopp**ing**, put → putt**ing** |
| BUT: verbs ending in -w, -x, -y: don't double | show → show**ing**, fix → fix**ing**, play → play**ing** |
| Two-syllable verbs with stress on last syllable: double last consonant | begin → beginn**ing**, prefer → preferr**ing**, forget → forgett**ing** |
| Two-syllable verbs with stress on first syllable: don't double | open → open**ing**, listen → listen**ing**, visit → visit**ing** |

## Questions - Preguntas

### Yes/No Questions - Preguntas Sí/No

**Structure - Estructura:**
**Am/Is/Are + subject + verb-ing?**

| To Be | Subject | Verb-ing | Example | Answer |
|:-----:|:-------:|:--------:|:--------|:-------|
| Are | you | working | **Are** you **working** now? | Yes, I am. / No, I'm not. |
| Is | he | working | **Is** he **working** today? | Yes, he is. / No, he isn't. |
| Is | she | studying | **Is** she **studying**? | Yes, she is. / No, she isn't. |
| Are | they | coming | **Are** they **coming** to the party? | Yes, they are. / No, they aren't. |
| Is | it | raining | **Is** it **raining**? | Yes, it is. / No, it isn't. |

## When to Use - Cuándo Usar

### 1. Actions Happening Now - Acciones Ocurriendo Ahora

At this moment, right now:

| Español | English |
|:-------:|:-------:|
| Estoy estudiando ahora. | I **am studying** now. |
| Ella está comiendo en este momento. | She **is eating** at the moment. |
| Están jugando al fútbol ahora mismo. | They **are playing** football right now. |
| Estoy hablando por teléfono. | I **am talking** on the phone. |

**Time expressions:**
- now
- right now
- at the moment
- at present
- currently
- today (when talking about the current day)

### 2. Temporary Situations - Situaciones Temporales

Things happening around now, but not permanent:

| Español | English |
|:-------:|:-------:|
| Estoy viviendo con mis padres (temporalmente). | I **am living** with my parents. |
| Ella está trabajando en un proyecto. | She **is working** on a project. |
| Están estudiando para los exámenes. | They **are studying** for exams. |
| Estoy leyendo un libro interesante. | I **am reading** an interesting book. |

**Time expressions:**
- this week / this month / this year
- these days
- at the moment
- for now
- temporarily

### 3. Future Arrangements - Planes Futuros (Ya Organizados)

Plans that are already arranged:

| Español | English |
|:-------:|:-------:|
| Voy a cenar con María mañana. | I **am having** dinner with Maria tomorrow. |
| Ellos se van de vacaciones el lunes. | They **are going** on holiday on Monday. |
| ¿Qué estás haciendo este fin de semana? | What **are** you **doing** this weekend? |
| Nos vamos a las 8. | We **are leaving** at 8. |

**Time expressions:**
- tomorrow
- tonight
- next week / next month
- this weekend
- on Monday
- at 5pm

### 4. Annoying Habits - Hábitos Molestos (con "always")

With "always" to show annoyance:

| Español | English |
|:-------:|:-------:|
| ¡Siempre está quejándose! | He **is always complaining**! |
| Siempre llega tarde. | She **is always arriving** late! |
| Siempre están haciendo ruido. | They **are always making** noise! |
| Siempre pierdes tus llaves. | You **are always losing** your keys! |

## Present Simple vs Present Continuous

### Comparison - Comparación:

| Present Simple | Present Continuous |
|:--------------|:-------------------|
| **Permanent / Regular** | **Temporary / Now** |
| I **work** in a bank. (my job) | I **am working** on a report. (now) |
| She **lives** in Madrid. (permanent) | She **is living** with friends. (temporary) |
| They **play** tennis. (hobby) | They **are playing** tennis. (right now) |
| He **wears** glasses. (always) | He **is wearing** a blue shirt. (today) |
| **Facts / Habits** | **Actions in progress** |
| The sun **rises** in the east. (fact) | The sun **is rising**. (happening now) |
| I **go** to the gym on Mondays. (habit) | I **am going** to the gym. (on my way now) |

### Key Difference:

**Present Simple:**
- ✅ I **work** here. (my job, permanent)
- ✅ I **drink** coffee every day. (habit)

**Present Continuous:**
- ✅ I **am working** now. (at this moment)
- ✅ I **am drinking** coffee. (right now, cup in hand)

## Stative Verbs - Verbos de Estado

Some verbs are NOT usually used in continuous form because they describe states, not actions:

### Mental States - Estados Mentales:

| Verb | Example |
|:----:|:--------|
| know | I **know** the answer. ❌ I am knowing |
| understand | I **understand** you. ❌ I am understanding |
| believe | I **believe** you. ❌ I am believing |
| think (opinion) | I **think** it's good. ❌ I am thinking |
| remember | I **remember** her. ❌ I am remembering |
| forget | I **forget** his name. ❌ I am forgetting |

### Emotions - Emociones:

| Verb | Example |
|:----:|:--------|
| love | I **love** chocolate. ❌ I am loving |
| like | I **like** pizza. ❌ I am liking |
| hate | I **hate** rain. ❌ I am hating |
| want | I **want** coffee. ❌ I am wanting |
| need | I **need** help. ❌ I am needing |
| prefer | I **prefer** tea. ❌ I am preferring |

### Possession - Posesión:

| Verb | Example |
|:----:|:--------|
| have (possess) | I **have** a car. ❌ I am having |
| own | She **owns** a house. ❌ She is owning |
| belong | It **belongs** to me. ❌ It is belonging |

### Senses - Sentidos:

| Verb | Example |
|:----:|:--------|
| see | I **see** you. ❌ I am seeing |
| hear | I **hear** music. ❌ I am hearing |
| smell | It **smells** good. ❌ It is smelling |
| taste | It **tastes** nice. ❌ It is tasting |

### Others - Otros:

| Verb | Example |
|:----:|:--------|
| be | I **am** happy. ❌ I am being happy (except for behavior) |
| seem | It **seems** difficult. ❌ It is seeming |
| appear | She **appears** sad. ❌ She is appearing |
| contain | It **contains** sugar. ❌ It is containing |
| consist | It **consists** of... ❌ It is consisting |

### Exceptions - Excepciones:

Some verbs can be used in continuous form with different meanings:

| Verb | Stative (Simple) | Active (Continuous) |
|:----:|:----------------|:--------------------|
| **think** | I **think** it's good. (opinion) | I **am thinking** about it. (mental process) |
| **have** | I **have** a car. (possession) | I **am having** lunch. (eating) / I **am having** fun. (experiencing) |
| **see** | I **see** you. (vision) | I **am seeing** the doctor. (meeting) |
| **be** | She **is** nice. (characteristic) | She **is being** rude. (behaving, temporary) |
| **taste** | It **tastes** good. (has a flavor) | I **am tasting** the soup. (testing the flavor) |
| **smell** | It **smells** nice. (has an odor) | I **am smelling** the flowers. (actively sniffing) |

## Complete Examples - Ejemplos Completos

```
A: Hi! What are you doing?

A: I'm cooking dinner. Are you busy tomorrow?
```

```
football. It's a beautiful sunny day and everyone is enjoying the weather.
```

## Time Expressions Summary - Resumen de Expresiones de Tiempo

### Present Continuous:
- now
- right now
- at the moment
- at present
- currently
- today (for actions happening today)
- this week/month/year (for temporary situations)
- Look! / Listen! (calling attention to something happening)

### Present Simple:
- every day/week/month/year
- always, usually, often, sometimes, never
- on Mondays, at weekends
- in the morning/afternoon/evening

---

**Tip**: To know if you should use Present Continuous, ask yourself: "Is this happening RIGHT NOW or is it TEMPORARY?" If yes, use Present Continuous. If it's a habit, fact, or permanent situation, use Present Simple!
