# Prepositions of Time and Place

## AT, IN, ON - The Big Three

These are the most important prepositions in English. Learning when to use each one is essential.

---

## Prepositions of PLACE (Lugar)

### AT - Punto específico / Lugar concreto

Use **AT** for specific points or locations:

| English | Spanish | Example |
|---------|---------|---------|
| at home | en casa | I am **at home** |
| at work | en el trabajo | She is **at work** |
| at school | en el colegio/escuela | The children are **at school** |
| at university | en la universidad | He studies **at university** |
| at the station | en la estación | Meet me **at the station** |
| at the airport | en el aeropuerto | I'll pick you up **at the airport** |
| at the bus stop | en la parada de autobús | Wait **at the bus stop** |
| at the door | en la puerta | Someone is **at the door** |
| at the top | en la cima/arriba | The cat is **at the top** of the tree |
| at the bottom | en el fondo/abajo | The answer is **at the bottom** of the page |
| at the end | al final | Turn left **at the end** of the street |
| at the corner | en la esquina | The shop is **at the corner** |
| at reception | en recepción | Ask **at reception** |
| at the doctor's | en el médico | I was **at the doctor's** yesterday |
| at the dentist's | en el dentista | She is **at the dentist's** |
| at a party | en una fiesta | I met him **at a party** |
| at a concert | en un concierto | We were **at a concert** |
| at a wedding | en una boda | They met **at a wedding** |

**Key idea**: AT = a specific point or location (como un punto en un mapa)

---

### IN - Dentro de / Espacios cerrados o áreas

Use **IN** for enclosed spaces, areas, countries, cities:

| English | Spanish | Example |
|---------|---------|---------|
| in a room | en una habitación | He is **in** his room |
| in a building | en un edificio | The office is **in** that building |
| in a car | en un coche | She is waiting **in** the car |
| in a taxi | en un taxi | I'm **in** a taxi now |
| in a box | en una caja | The present is **in** the box |
| in the kitchen | en la cocina | Mum is **in** the kitchen |
| in the garden | en el jardín | The dog is **in** the garden |
| in the park | en el parque | Let's meet **in** the park |
| in the city center | en el centro | The hotel is **in** the city center |
| in the street | en la calle | I saw him **in** the street |
| in a line/queue | en una fila/cola | We waited **in** line for hours |
| in hospital | en el hospital | My grandfather is **in** hospital |
| in prison | en prisión | He is **in** prison |
| in bed | en la cama | She is still **in** bed |
| in the water | en el agua | The children are **in** the water |
| in the sea | en el mar | We swam **in** the sea |
| in the pool | en la piscina | He is **in** the pool |
| in the sky | en el cielo | Birds fly **in** the sky |
| in a photo | en una foto | Who is **in** this photo? |
| in a book | en un libro | I read it **in** a book |
| in the newspaper | en el periódico | I saw it **in** the newspaper |

#### Countries, Cities, Continents

| English | Example |
|---------|---------|
| in + country | I live **in** Spain |
| in + city | She works **in** Madrid |
| in + continent | The biggest desert is **in** Africa |
| in + neighborhood | They live **in** Malasaña |

**Key idea**: IN = inside an enclosed space or area (dentro de algo)

---

### ON - Sobre una superficie / Contacto

Use **ON** for surfaces, floors, transport (public), streets:

| English | Spanish | Example |
|---------|---------|---------|
| on the table | en/sobre la mesa | The book is **on** the table |
| on the floor | en el suelo | The cat is sleeping **on** the floor |
| on the wall | en la pared | There's a picture **on** the wall |
| on the ceiling | en el techo | A fly is **on** the ceiling |
| on the shelf | en la estantería | The cups are **on** the shelf |
| on the bed | en la cama (encima) | The clothes are **on** the bed |
| on the chair | en la silla | Don't sit **on** that chair |
| on the sofa | en el sofá | He's sitting **on** the sofa |
| on the ground | en el suelo (exterior) | The ball is **on** the ground |
| on the grass | en la hierba | They were sitting **on** the grass |
| on the beach | en la playa | We spent the day **on** the beach |
| on the coast | en la costa | The town is **on** the coast |
| on the river | en el río | Oxford is **on** the River Thames |
| on an island | en una isla | They live **on** an island |
| on the first floor | en el primer piso | My office is **on** the first floor |
| on the left/right | a la izquierda/derecha | The bank is **on** the left |

#### Public Transport

| Transport | Example |
|-----------|---------|
| on a bus | I'm **on** the bus |
| on a train | She's **on** the train |
| on a plane | We're **on** the plane |
| on a ship/boat | They traveled **on** a ship |
| on the metro | I'll be **on** the metro |
| on a bike | He goes to work **on** his bike |
| on a motorbike | She arrived **on** a motorbike |
| on foot | I go to school **on** foot |

> **Exception**: Use **IN** for cars and taxis: "in a car", "in a taxi"

#### Streets and Roads

| Type | Example |
|------|---------|
| on + street name | I live **on** Oxford Street |
| on the corner of | The café is **on** the corner of Main Street |
| on the way | I'll call you **on** the way |

**Key idea**: ON = touching a surface or line (sobre una superficie)

## AT vs IN - Common Confusions

| AT (punto específico) | IN (dentro de) |
|-----------------------|----------------|
| at the hospital (visitando) | in hospital (como paciente) |
| at school (estudiante ahí) | in the school building (dentro del edificio) |
| at home | in my house |
| at the cinema (evento) | in the cinema (dentro) |
| at the restaurant | in the restaurant (inside) |

## Prepositions of TIME (Tiempo)

### AT - Horas y momentos específicos

| Use | Example |
|-----|---------|
| at + hour | at 5 o'clock, at 10:30 |
| at night | I work at night |
| at midnight | The party ends at midnight |
| at noon/midday | We have lunch at noon |
| at the weekend (UK) | I relax at the weekend |
| at Christmas | We visit family at Christmas |
| at Easter | They travel at Easter |
| at the moment | I'm busy at the moment |
| at the same time | They arrived at the same time |
| at the beginning/end | At the end of the movie... |

---

### IN - Períodos más largos

| Use | Example |
|-----|---------|
| in + month | in January, in December |
| in + year | in 2024, in 1990 |
| in + season | in summer, in winter |
| in + century | in the 21st century |
| in the morning | I exercise in the morning |
| in the afternoon | I work in the afternoon |
| in the evening | I relax in the evening |
| in + time period | I'll be ready in 5 minutes |
| in the past | In the past, people traveled by horse |
| in the future | In the future, cars will fly |
| in a week/month/year | I'll see you in a week |

> **Note**: "at night" but "in the morning/afternoon/evening"

---

### ON - Días y fechas

| Use | Example |
|-----|---------|
| on + day | on Monday, on Friday |
| on + date | on 25th December, on July 4th |
| on + specific day | on Christmas Day, on my birthday |
| on the weekend (US) | I rest on the weekend |
| on Monday morning | I have a meeting on Monday morning |
| on New Year's Eve | We have a party on New Year's Eve |

## Quick Reference Table

| | AT | IN | ON |
|---|----|----|-----|
| **Place** | Specific point | Enclosed space | Surface |
| | at home, at work | in a room, in Spain | on the table |
| **Time** | Specific time | Periods | Days/Dates |
| | at 5pm, at night | in January, in the morning | on Monday, on July 4th |
| **Transport** | - | in a car, in a taxi | on a bus, on a train |

## Common Mistakes

❌ I am **in** home → ✅ I am **at** home
❌ I am **at** the car → ✅ I am **in** the car
❌ She is **in** work → ✅ She is **at** work
❌ I live **at** Madrid → ✅ I live **in** Madrid
❌ The book is **in** the table → ✅ The book is **on** the table
❌ I saw him **at** the street → ✅ I saw him **in** the street
❌ I go **on** bus → ✅ I go **on** the bus / I go **by** bus
❌ **In** Monday I work → ✅ **On** Monday I work
❌ **On** the morning → ✅ **In** the morning
❌ **In** night → ✅ **At** night
❌ **On** Christmas → ✅ **At** Christmas
❌ **In** 5 o'clock → ✅ **At** 5 o'clock

## BY - Medio de transporte (sin artículo)

When talking about HOW you travel (method), use **BY** without article:

| English | Spanish |
|---------|---------|
| by car | en coche |
| by bus | en autobús |
| by train | en tren |
| by plane | en avión |
| by bike | en bici |
| by taxi | en taxi |
| by boat | en barco |
| by metro | en metro |

> **Exception**: "on foot" (NOT "by foot")

**Compare**:
- I go to work **by** bus (method - cómo voy)
- I'm **on** the bus right now (location - dónde estoy)

### Exercise 1: AT, IN, or ON?

1. I live _____ a small apartment _____ the third floor.
2. She works _____ a bank _____ the city center.
3. We met _____ a party _____ New Year's Eve.
4. The keys are _____ the table _____ the kitchen.
5. I'll see you _____ 7 o'clock _____ Monday evening.
6. He was born _____ Spain _____ 1990.
7. The children are playing _____ the garden.
8. My office is _____ the corner of Main Street.
9. I read about it _____ the newspaper.
10. She's waiting _____ the bus stop.

1. **in** a small apartment **on** the third floor

3. **at** a party **on** New Year's Eve
4. **on** the table **in** the kitchen
5. **at** 7 o'clock **on** Monday evening
6. **in** Spain **in** 1990
7. **in** the garden
8. **on** the corner of Main Street

2. She lives at Barcelona.

4. Put the book in the shelf.
5. I go to work on car.
6. We arrived in the airport at 10am.
7. The cat is under of the bed.
