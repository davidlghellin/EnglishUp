# Pronunciation - A2 Level

## Topics

### [001 - Spanish Speaker Mistakes](001-spanish-speaker-mistakes.md)
Common pronunciation errors and how to fix them:
- The "e" before S+consonant (e-Spain → Spain)
- The H sound (ello → hello)
- V vs B sounds
- -ED endings (/t/, /d/, /ɪd/)
- Short I vs Long EE (ship vs sheep)
- Word stress
- TH sounds
- Silent letters (could, walk, knife)

### [002 - American vs British English](002-american-vs-british.md)
Focus on American English pronunciation:
- R sounds (always pronounced)
- Flapped T (water → wader)
- Flat A sound (can't, dance)
- Common reductions (gonna, wanna, hafta)
- Word differences (schedule, vitamin)

---

**Tip**: Practice pronunciation for 5 minutes every day. Record yourself and compare with native speakers!
