# Ordering at Restaurants

## Polite Phrases for Eating Out

---

## Polite Ways to Order

### ❌ Informal / Rude vs ✅ Polite

| Avoid ❌ | Use instead ✅ |
|----------|---------------|
| Give me a burger | **I'd like** a burger, please |
| I want a coffee | **Could I have** a coffee, please? |
| Bring me the menu | **Could we have** the menu, please? |
| Water! | **Could we have** some water, please? |

### Key Phrases for Ordering

**Most polite (recommended):**
- **I'd like...** (I would like)
- **Could I have...?**
- **Could we have...?**
- **May I have...?**

**Also acceptable:**
- **Can I have...?**
- **I'll have...**

**Examples:**
- **I'd like** the chicken, please.
- **Could I have** a glass of water?
- **Can I have** the bill, please?
- **I'll have** the steak, medium rare.

## At the Restaurant - Step by Step

### 1. Arriving

| Waiter | You |
|--------|-----|
| Good evening. Do you have a reservation? | Yes, under [name] / No, do you have a table for two? |
| Table for how many? | For two, please |
| Would you like to sit inside or outside? | Inside/Outside, please |
| This way, please | Thank you |

### 2. Getting the Menu

| You | Waiter |
|-----|--------|
| **Could we have** the menu, please? | Of course. Here you are. |
| **Could we have** a few more minutes? | Take your time. |
| What do you recommend? | I recommend the... / The ... is very popular. |

### 3. Ordering Food

| Waiter | You |
|--------|-----|
| Are you ready to order? | Yes, **I'd like** the salmon, please. |
| What would you like? | **Could I have** the steak, please? |
| How would you like your steak? | Medium rare / Medium / Well done, please. |
| Would you like any sides? | Yes, **I'll have** the fries, please. |
| Anything else? | No, that's all, thank you. |

### 4. Ordering Drinks

| Waiter | You |
|--------|-----|
| Would you like something to drink? | **Could I have** a glass of red wine? |
| Still or sparkling water? | Still, please. / Sparkling, please. |
| Would you like ice? | Yes, please. / No, thank you. |

### 5. During the Meal

| Situation | What to say |
|-----------|-------------|
| Need something | Excuse me, **could we have** some more bread? |
| Wrong order | Sorry, I ordered the chicken, not the fish. |
| Something missing | Excuse me, I think we're missing a fork. |
| Compliment | This is delicious! |

### 6. Paying

| You | Waiter |
|-----|--------|
| **Could we have** the bill, please? | Of course. |
| **Can I pay** by card? | Yes, of course. / Sorry, cash only. |
| Is service included? | Yes, it is. / No, it isn't. |
| Keep the change | Thank you very much! |

## Talking About Prices

### Saying Something is Expensive

| Natural ✅ | Unnatural ❌ |
|-----------|-------------|
| It's **expensive** | ~~It's very cost~~ |
| It's **too expensive** | ~~It has much cost~~ |
| It's **pricey** (informal) | ~~It costs much~~ |
| It's **a bit pricey** | |
| It's **overpriced** | |
| It's **not cheap** | |

### Saying Something is Cheap

| Natural ✅ |
|-----------|
| It's **cheap** |
| It's **affordable** |
| It's **good value** |
| It's **a bargain** |
| It's **reasonable** |

### Examples

- The restaurant is nice, but it's **a bit pricey**.
- This place is great and **not too expensive**.
- The food is **good value for money**.
- That wine is **overpriced**. Let's get something else.
- The lunch menu is very **affordable**.

## Role Play Practice

### Dialogue Example

**Waiter:** Good evening. Do you have a reservation?

**You:** No, we don't. Do you have a table for two?

**Waiter:** Yes, of course. This way, please. Here are your menus.

**You:** Thank you. Could we have some water, please?

**Waiter:** Still or sparkling?

**You:** Still, please.

*[After a few minutes]*

**Waiter:** Are you ready to order?

**You:** Yes, I'd like the tomato soup as a starter, and then I'll have the grilled salmon, please.

**Waiter:** How would you like your salmon?

**You:** Medium, please.

**Waiter:** Would you like any sides?

**You:** Could I have a salad, please?

**Waiter:** Of course. Anything to drink?

**You:** Could I have a glass of white wine?

**Waiter:** Certainly.

*[After the meal]*

**You:** Excuse me, could we have the bill, please?

**Waiter:** Of course. Here you are.

**You:** Can I pay by card?

**Waiter:** Yes, of course.

**You:** Thank you. The food was delicious!

## Common Mistakes

| Wrong ❌ | Correct ✅ |
|----------|-----------|
| Give me a burger | **I'd like** a burger, please |
| I want pay | **I'd like to pay** / **Can I pay?** |
| The bill! | **Could we have the bill, please?** |
| More water! | **Could we have some more water?** |
| It's very cost | It's **expensive** / It's **pricey** |
