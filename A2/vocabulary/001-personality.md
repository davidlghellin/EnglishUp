## How to Use

### Describing yourself or others:
- **I am** friendly and talkative.
- **She is** very intelligent and creative.
- **He is** a kind and patient person.
- **They are** hardworking and responsible.

### Making sentences:
- My best friend is **funny** and **outgoing**.
- My boss is **serious** but **fair**.
- I try to be **honest** and **generous**.

## Practice

**Exercise**: Choose 5 adjectives that describe you and write sentences.

Example: *I am friendly and sociable. I like talking to people.*

---

**Tip**: You can use "very", "quite", or "a bit" to modify adjectives: She is **very** kind. He is **quite** shy. I am **a bit** lazy.
