## How to Use

### Describing hair:
- **He has** short black hair.
- **She has** long blonde hair.
- **I have** curly brown hair.
- **They have** straight hair.

### More detailed descriptions:
- She has **long, straight, blonde** hair.
- He has **short, curly, black** hair with a **beard**.
- My sister has **medium-length, wavy, brown** hair.
- I have **shoulder-length, straight, dark brown** hair with **bangs**.

### Talking about facial hair:
- He has **a beard** and **a moustache**.
- My father is **clean-shaven**.
- He has **a goatee**.
- He needs to shave - he has **stubble**.

## Common Expressions

| Español | English |
|:-------:|:-------:|
| Cortarse el pelo | To get a haircut / To cut one's hair |
| Teñirse el pelo | To dye one's hair |
| Peinarse | To brush/comb one's hair |
| Recogerse el pelo | To tie one's hair up |
| Dejarse crecer el pelo | To grow one's hair |
| Dejarse crecer la barba | To grow a beard |

### Examples:
- I need **to get a haircut**.
- She **dyed her hair** red.
- I usually **tie my hair up** when I exercise.
- He is **growing a beard**.
