## How to Use

### Basic descriptions:
- **He is** tall and slim.
- **She is** short and thin.
- **I am** medium height.
- **They are** young and athletic.

### Detailed descriptions:
- He is **tall and muscular** with **broad shoulders**.
- She is **medium height and slim** with **long legs**.
- My grandfather is **elderly** with **grey hair** and **wrinkles**.
- The baby has **big blue eyes** and **rosy cheeks**.

### Complete person description:
```
My best friend is tall and slim. She has an oval face with high cheekbones.
She has big brown eyes and a beautiful smile. She has long, straight, black
hair. She usually wears glasses. She is very attractive and elegant.
```

### Using "look" and "look like":
- **She looks** young for her age.
- **He looks** tired today.
- **You look** great!
- **She looks like** her mother.
- **What does he look like?** - He's tall with short brown hair.

## Practice

**Exercise 1**: Describe yourself physically (5-7 sentences).

**Exercise 2**: Describe a family member or friend. Include:
- Height and build
- Age appearance
- Face features
- Eyes and hair
- Overall impression

Example: *My sister is medium height and slim. She is young, about 25 years old. She has an oval face with green eyes and freckles. She has long, wavy, red hair. She is very pretty and always smiling.*

---

**Tip**: When describing someone, start with general features (height, build, age) then move to specific details (face, eyes, hair).
