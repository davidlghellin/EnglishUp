## How to Use - Cómo Usar

### Wearing clothes - Present Continuous:
**Structure**: Subject + **am/is/are wearing** + clothes

- I **am wearing** a blue t-shirt and jeans.
- She **is wearing** a red dress.
- He **is wearing** a black suit and tie.
- They **are wearing** shorts and flip-flops.

### What you usually wear - Present Simple:
- I **wear** jeans every day.
- She **wears** glasses.
- He **wears** a suit to work.
- I usually **wear** trainers.

### Getting dressed:
- **Put on** - ponerse: I **put on** my coat.
- **Take off** - quitarse: She **took off** her shoes.
- **Try on** - probarse: Can I **try on** this dress?
- **Get dressed** - vestirse: I **get dressed** quickly in the morning.

## Complete Outfit Descriptions

### Example 1 - Casual:
```
Today I am wearing a white t-shirt, blue jeans, and white trainers.
I'm also wearing a black hoodie because it's cold. I have my backpack
with me.
```

### Example 2 - Formal:
```
She is wearing an elegant black dress with high heels. She is also
wearing a silver necklace and earrings. She looks very smart.
```

### Example 3 - Summer:
```
He is wearing a pair of shorts and a striped t-shirt. He's also
wearing sunglasses and flip-flops. It's perfect for the beach!
```

## Common Expressions

| Español | English |
|:-------:|:-------:|
| Quedarle bien | To suit someone / To fit |
| Ir bien con | To go with / To match |
| Probarse ropa | To try on clothes |
| Ir de compras | To go shopping |
| Estar a la moda | To be in fashion |

### Examples:
- This dress **suits** you! (Te queda bien)
- These shoes **don't fit** me. (No me quedan bien de talla)
- This belt **matches** your shoes. (Combina con)
- I need **to try on** these jeans.
- She is always **in fashion**.

## Practice

**Exercise 1**: Describe what you are wearing right now.

Example: *I am wearing a grey sweater, black jeans, and brown boots. I'm also wearing a watch and a scarf.*

**Exercise 2**: Describe your favorite outfit.

Example: *My favorite outfit is a white shirt with dark blue jeans and black leather shoes. I also like to wear a brown belt and my silver watch.*

**Exercise 3**: Describe what someone near you is wearing.

---

**Tip**: Use "a pair of" for items that come in pairs: **a pair of** jeans, **a pair of** shoes, **a pair of** gloves, **a pair of** shorts.
