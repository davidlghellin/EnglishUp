## How to Use - Present Continuous for Actions Happening Now

**Structure**: Subject + **am/is/are** + verb**-ing**

- I **am traveling** to Spain. (Estoy viajando)
- She **is packing** her suitcase. (Está haciendo la maleta)
- We **are boarding** the plane. (Estamos embarcando)
- They **are visiting** the museum. (Están visitando)
- He **is taking** photos. (Está sacando fotos)

## Practice

**Exercise 1**: Describe your typical morning routine using 5-7 action verbs.

Example: *I wake up at 7am. I get up and have a shower. Then I get dressed and have breakfast. I leave home at 8am and go to work.*

**Exercise 2**: Write about a trip you took. Use travel vocabulary and past tense.

Example: *Last summer, I traveled to Italy. I booked a hotel in Rome. I visited the Colosseum and took many photos. I also went to the beach and swam in the sea.*

**Exercise 3**: Describe what you are doing right now using Present Continuous.

Example: *I am sitting at my desk. I am studying English. I am reading about travel vocabulary.*

---

**Tip**: For the Present Continuous, remember these spelling rules:
- Most verbs: add **-ing** (work → working)
- Verbs ending in -e: remove -e, add **-ing** (make → making)
- Short verbs ending in consonant-vowel-consonant: double the last consonant (swim → swimming, run → running)
