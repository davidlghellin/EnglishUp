## Useful Verbs with Chores

### DO vs MAKE

**DO** - for tasks/work:
- do the dishes
- do the laundry
- do the cleaning
- do the shopping
- do the ironing
- do the housework

**MAKE** - for creating/preparing:
- make the bed
- make dinner
- make a mess (hacer un desastre)

## Talking About Responsibilities

### Who does what?

| Question | Answer |
|----------|--------|
| Who does the cooking? | My mum does the cooking |
| Who takes out the rubbish? | I take out the rubbish |
| Who is in charge of...? | My dad is in charge of the garden |
| Whose turn is it to...? | It's your turn to wash the dishes |

### Phrases for Sharing Tasks

- It's my/your turn to... (Es mi/tu turno de...)
- Can you help me with...? (¿Puedes ayudarme con...?)
- I'll do... if you do... (Yo haré... si tú haces...)
- We take turns doing... (Nos turnamos para hacer...)
- We share the housework (Compartimos las tareas del hogar)

## Example Sentences

**Present Simple (routines):**
- I **do the dishes** every evening.
- My brother **takes out the rubbish** on Tuesdays.
- We **share** the housework in my family.

**Present Continuous (now):**
- I'm **vacuuming** the living room right now.
- She's **ironing** her clothes for tomorrow.
- They're **cleaning** the house for the party.

**Past Simple (yesterday):**
- I **did the laundry** yesterday.
- She **cleaned** the bathroom this morning.
- We **mowed the lawn** last weekend.

**Going to (plans):**
- I'm **going to clean** my room later.
- We're **going to do** the shopping tomorrow.
- He's **going to fix** the broken window.

## Practice Exercises

### Exercise 1: Match the verb with the task
Match the verbs with what you clean/do:

1. vacuum _____    a) the dishes
2. mow _____       b) the bed
3. do _____        c) the carpet
4. make _____      d) the lawn
5. hang out _____  e) the washing

<details>
<summary>Answers</summary>

1-c, 2-d, 3-a, 4-b, 5-e
</details>

### Exercise 2: DO or MAKE?

1. _____ the bed
2. _____ the laundry
3. _____ dinner
4. _____ the dishes
5. _____ a mess
6. _____ the housework

<details>
<summary>Answers</summary>

1. make, 2. do, 3. make, 4. do, 5. make, 6. do
</details>

### Exercise 3: Write about your household responsibilities

Write 5-8 sentences about:
- What chores do you do at home?
- How often do you do them?
- Who else helps with the housework?
- What chore do you like/hate?

**Example:**
```
In my house, I help with the housework. I make my bed every morning and
I do the dishes after dinner. Once a week, I vacuum my bedroom and clean
the bathroom. My mum does the cooking and my dad mows the lawn. I hate
ironing, but I don't mind doing the laundry. We all help to keep the
house clean.
```

---

**Tip**: Learn these phrases in context! Next time you do a chore at home, say what you're doing in English: "I'm washing the dishes" or "I need to take out the rubbish."
