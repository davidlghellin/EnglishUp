## DO vs MAKE

This is confusing for Spanish speakers! Here's the rule:

### DO = activities, tasks, work
- do homework, do housework, do exercise, do the shopping

### MAKE = create, produce something
- make breakfast, make a mistake, make a decision, make friends

### Common Confusions

| Correct | Incorrect |
|---------|-----------|
| **make** a mistake | ~~do a mistake~~ |
| **do** homework | ~~make homework~~ |
| **make** money | ~~do money~~ |
| **do** exercise | ~~make exercise~~ |
| **make** a decision | ~~do a decision~~ |
| **do** the dishes | ~~make the dishes~~ |

## Practice Exercises

### Exercise 1: Complete with GET, HAVE, GO, TAKE, MAKE, or DO

1. I _______ up at 7am every day.
2. Let's _______ a coffee together.
3. She _______ to work by train.
4. Can you _______ a photo of us?
5. I need to _______ the shopping.
6. Don't _______ so much noise!
7. We're _______ on holiday next month.
8. I _______ breakfast at 8am.

<details>
<summary>Answers</summary>

1. get
2. have
3. goes
4. take
5. do
6. make
7. going
8. have
</details>
