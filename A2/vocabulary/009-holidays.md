## Holiday Activities

### At the Beach

| English | Spanish | Example |
|---------|---------|---------|
| sunbathe | tomar el sol | I love to **sunbathe** on the beach |
| swim in the sea | nadar en el mar | We **swam in the sea** every day |
| build sandcastles | hacer castillos de arena | The kids **built sandcastles** |
| go snorkeling | hacer snorkel | We **went snorkeling** and saw fish |
| go surfing | hacer surf | He **goes surfing** every summer |
| relax by the pool | relajarse en la piscina | I just want to **relax by the pool** |
| get a tan | ponerse moreno | She **got a tan** on holiday |
| get sunburned | quemarse con el sol | I **got sunburned** on the first day |

## Practice Exercises

### Exercise 1: Match the activity to the holiday type

1. sunbathe, swim, snorkel → _______________
2. visit museums, explore the city → _______________
3. hike, climb, kayak → _______________
4. sleep in a tent, make a campfire → _______________

<details>
<summary>Answers</summary>

1. beach holiday
2. city break / sightseeing trip
3. adventure holiday
4. camping holiday
</details>

### Exercise 2: Complete the sentences

1. We _______ at a lovely hotel by the beach.
2. I need to _______ my bags before the trip.
3. Did you _______ any photos of the Eiffel Tower?
4. We _______ sightseeing in Barcelona.
5. Don't forget to _______ out by 11am!

<details>
<summary>Answers</summary>

1. stayed
2. pack
3. take
4. went
5. check
</details>

### Exercise 3: Describe your last holiday

Write 5 sentences about a holiday using vocabulary from this lesson:
- Where did you go?
- Where did you stay?
- What did you do?
- What was the weather like?
- Did you enjoy it?

---

**Tip**: When talking about holidays in the past, use Past Simple: "We **went** to Spain", "We **stayed** at a hotel", "We **visited** the beach every day".
