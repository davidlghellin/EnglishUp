## Practice Exercises

### Exercise 1: Put in order

Number these steps 1-8 in the correct order:

___ Go through security
___ Board the plane
___ Check in at the desk
___ Collect your luggage
___ Go to the gate
___ Go through passport control
___ Arrive at the airport
___ The plane takes off

<details>
<summary>Answers</summary>

1. Arrive at the airport
2. Check in at the desk
3. Go through security
4. Go through passport control
5. Go to the gate
6. Board the plane
7. The plane takes off
8. Collect your luggage (after landing)
</details>

### Exercise 2: Complete the dialogue

A: Good morning. Can I see your **p________** and **b________ c________**, please?

B: Yes, here you are.

A: Are you checking any **l________**?

B: Yes, one **s________**.

A: Would you like a **w________** seat or an **a________** seat?

B: Window, please.

A: Here's your boarding pass. Your **g________** is B12. **B________** starts at 10:30.

B: Thank you. Is the flight **o__ t________**?

A: Yes, it is. Have a good flight!

<summary>Answers</summary>

passport, booking confirmation, luggage, suitcase, window, aisle, gate, Boarding, on time
</details>
