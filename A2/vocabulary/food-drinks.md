## Cooking Vocabulary

### Soups and Liquids

| Word | Meaning | Example |
|------|---------|---------|
| **broth** /brɒθ/ | caldo (el líquido) | The chicken **broth** is very hot |
| **soup** | sopa (con ingredientes) | I made vegetable **soup** |
| **stock** | caldo base para cocinar | Use beef **stock** for the sauce |
| **stew** | guiso, estofado | Beef **stew** with potatoes |

> **Broth vs Soup**: Broth is just the liquid. Soup has vegetables, meat, noodles, etc.

### Cooking Methods

| Word | Spanish | Example |
|------|---------|---------|
| **grill** | asar a la parrilla | I like to **grill** steaks |
| **barbecue (BBQ)** | asar (general) | We **barbecued** chicken yesterday |
| **roast** | asar al horno | **Roast** the vegetables for 30 minutes |
| **bake** | hornear | She **baked** a cake |
| **fry** | freír | **Fry** the onions until golden |
| **boil** | hervir | **Boil** the water first |
| **steam** | cocinar al vapor | **Steam** the vegetables |
| **sauté** | saltear | **Sauté** the garlic in olive oil |
| **simmer** | cocinar a fuego lento | Let it **simmer** for 2 hours |

> **Note**: In English, "barbecue" is used broadly for any grilled/roasted meat, even in the oven. It doesn't require BBQ sauce or open flames.

## Practice Exercises

### Exercise 1: Choose the correct word

1. The liquid from cooking chicken is called _______ (soup / broth)
2. The steak was very _______, not dry at all (juicy / salty)
3. I need to _______ the onions in butter (sauté / boil)
4. This dish is very _______, full of great flavors (bland / flavorful)
5. The _______ took our order quickly (chef / waiter)

<details>
<summary>Answers</summary>

1. broth
2. juicy
3. sauté
4. flavorful
5. waiter
</details>
