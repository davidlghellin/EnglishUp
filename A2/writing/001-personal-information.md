# Personal Information

## Writing About Yourself

At A2 level, you should be able to write simple texts about yourself, your family, and your daily life.

## Key Vocabulary

- **Personal details**: name, age, nationality, job, address
- **Family**: parents, siblings, children, husband/wife
- **Interests**: hobbies, likes, dislikes

## Useful Phrases

- My name is...
- I am... years old
- I live in...
- I work as a...
- I study...
- My family consists of...
- In my free time, I like to...
- My hobbies are...

## Example Text

```
My name is Maria and I am 28 years old. I am from Spain and I live in Madrid
with my family. I work as a teacher in a primary school.

I have one brother and one sister. My brother is older than me and my sister
is younger. We live with our parents in a small apartment in the city center.

In my free time, I like to read books and watch movies. I also enjoy cooking
and spending time with my friends. On weekends, I usually go to the park with
my dog.
```

## Writing Practice

### Exercise 1: Complete the sentences
Write about yourself using these prompts:

1. My name is ________________
2. I am ________________ years old
3. I live in ________________
4. I work/study ________________
5. My family ________________
6. In my free time ________________

### Exercise 2: Write a short paragraph
Write 5-7 sentences about yourself. Include:
- Your name and age
- Where you live
- Your job or studies
- Your hobbies

---

**Tip**: Keep it simple! Use short sentences and focus on accuracy, not length.
