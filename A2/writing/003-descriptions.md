# Descriptions

## Describing People, Places, and Things

Learn to write clear descriptions using adjectives and simple structures.

## Describing People

### Physical Appearance
- **Height**: tall, short, medium height
- **Build**: slim, thin, average, heavy
- **Hair**: long, short, curly, straight, blonde, brown, black, red, gray
- **Eyes**: blue, brown, green, gray
- **Age**: young, middle-aged, old, elderly

### Personality
- friendly, kind, nice, funny, serious, quiet, talkative, shy, confident

### Example

```
My best friend is called Tom. He is tall and slim with short brown hair
and blue eyes. He is 25 years old.

Tom is a very friendly and funny person. He always makes me laugh. He is
also very kind and helpful. He works as a nurse in a hospital.
```

## Describing Places

### Useful Adjectives
- **Size**: big, small, large, tiny, huge
- **Appearance**: beautiful, ugly, modern, old, clean, dirty
- **Atmosphere**: quiet, noisy, peaceful, busy, crowded

### Example

```
My hometown is a small city in the north of Spain. It has about 50,000
people. The city is very old and has many historical buildings.

The center is beautiful with narrow streets and old churches. There is a
big park near my house where people go to relax. I like living here because
it is quiet and safe.
```

## Describing Things

### Useful Adjectives
- **Size**: big, small, large, tiny
- **Color**: red, blue, green, yellow, black, white
- **Quality**: good, bad, new, old, expensive, cheap
- **Material**: wooden, metal, plastic, glass

### Example

```
I have a new bicycle. It is red and black and it is very beautiful. The
bicycle is quite expensive, but it is good quality.

It has 21 gears and it is very fast. I use it every day to go to work.
I really love my new bike!
```

## Writing Practice

### Exercise 1: Describe a person
Write a description of:
- A family member
- A friend
- A famous person you admire

Include: physical appearance and personality (5-7 sentences)

### Exercise 2: Describe a place
Write about:
- Your city or town
- Your favorite place
- A place you visited

Include: location, size, appearance, what you can do there (6-8 sentences)

### Exercise 3: Describe an object
Write about:
- Your phone or computer
- Your favorite possession
- Something you bought recently

Include: what it is, what it looks like, why you like it (4-6 sentences)

---

**Tip**: Use a variety of adjectives to make your descriptions more interesting. But don't use too many in one sentence!
