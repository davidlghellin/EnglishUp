# Simple Narratives

## Writing About Past Events and Experiences

Learn to write simple stories about things that happened to you.

## Key Grammar

- **Past Simple**: for completed actions (I went, I saw, I did)
- **Time expressions**: yesterday, last week, last year, ago, in 2020
- **Sequencing words**: first, then, after that, finally

## Structure of a Simple Narrative

1. **Introduction**: When and where did it happen?
2. **Main events**: What happened? (Use sequencing words)
3. **Conclusion**: How did it end? How did you feel?

## Useful Time Expressions

- **Yesterday**: yesterday morning, yesterday afternoon
- **Last**: last weekend, last month, last summer, last year
- **Ago**: two days ago, a week ago, three months ago
- **Specific dates**: on Monday, in July, in 2023

## Example 1: A Day Trip

```
Last Saturday, I went to the beach with my friends. The weather was beautiful
and sunny.

First, we arrived at the beach at 10am and found a good place to sit. Then, we
went swimming in the sea. The water was very cold but refreshing. After that,
we had lunch at a small restaurant near the beach. The food was delicious.

In the afternoon, we played volleyball and walked along the beach. Finally, we
went home at 6pm.

I had a wonderful day and I felt very happy and relaxed.
```

## Example 2: A Special Memory

```
I remember my first day at university. It was in September 2019. I was very
nervous but also excited.

When I arrived, I didn't know anyone. I went to my first class and sat at the
back. A girl sat next to me and said hello. Her name was Anna and she was also
nervous.

We talked during the break and discovered that we had many things in common.
After class, we had lunch together in the cafeteria.

That was five years ago, and now Anna is my best friend. I'm so glad I met her
on that first day!
```

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

❌ Yesterday I go to the cinema (Wrong tense)
✅ Yesterday I went to the cinema

❌ I have visited Paris last year (Wrong tense)
✅ I visited Paris last year

❌ First, I went to the shop. After that I go home (Mixed tenses)
✅ First, I went to the shop. After that I went home

## Writing Practice

### Exercise 1: Your last weekend
Write about what you did last weekend (6-8 sentences):
- Where did you go?
- Who did you see?
- What did you do?
- How did you feel?

### Exercise 2: A memorable trip
Write about a trip or holiday you took (8-10 sentences):
- When and where did you go?
- Who did you go with?
- What did you do there?
- What was the best part?

### Exercise 3: Your first...
Write about a "first" experience (7-9 sentences):
- Your first day at school/work
- Your first time traveling alone
- The first time you did something new

Remember to use:
- Past simple tense
- Time expressions
- Sequencing words

---

**Tip**: Before you write, make a simple plan. List the main events in order, then add details!
