# Opinion Essay

## How to Write a Formal Opinion Essay

---

## Important Rules

1. **No contractions** → don't ❌ → do not ✅ / can't ❌ → cannot ✅
2. **No informal language** → "stuff", "a lot of", "cool" ❌
3. **Use formal connectors** → not just "and", "but", "because"
4. **Do not use "I think" in every sentence** → use alternatives (see below)

---

## Essay Structure (4 Paragraphs)

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│  TITLE                                       │
├─────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│  1. INTRODUCTION (Paragraph 1)               │
│     Present the topic with a general comment │
├─────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│  2. BODY - Opinion 1 (Paragraph 2)           │
│     + reasons / advantages                   │
├─────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│  3. BODY - Opinion 2 (Paragraph 3)           │
│     + reasons / advantages                   │
├─────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│  4. CONCLUSION (Paragraph 4)                 │
│     Writer's personal opinion                │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

---

## Formal Connectors

### To Add Ideas (Además)

| Connector | Example |
|-----------|---------|
| **Moreover** | Online shopping is cheaper. **Moreover**, it saves time. |
| **In addition** | You can compare prices. **In addition**, there are many reviews. |
| **Furthermore** | It is convenient. **Furthermore**, delivery is often free. |
| **Also** | You can shop at any time. It is **also** more comfortable. |

### To Contrast (Sin embargo / Pero)

| Connector | Example |
|-----------|---------|
| **However** | Online shopping is fast. **However**, you cannot try the clothes on. |
| **On the other hand** | It is cheaper. **On the other hand**, you have to wait for delivery. |
| **Nevertheless** | There are risks. **Nevertheless**, most people prefer it. |
| **Although** | **Although** it is convenient, some people prefer physical stores. |

### To Give Examples (Por ejemplo)

| Connector | Example |
|-----------|---------|
| **For example** | There are many advantages. **For example**, you do not have to leave home. |
| **For instance** | Some products are cheaper online. **For instance**, electronics. |
| **Such as** | Physical stores offer services **such as** personal assistance. |

### To Order Ideas (Primero, Segundo...)

| Connector | Use |
|-----------|-----|
| **First / Firstly** | First point |
| **Second / Secondly** | Second point |
| **Third / Thirdly** | Third point |
| **Finally** | Last point |

### To Give Your Opinion (NOT just "I think"!)

| Expression | Formality | Example |
|------------|-----------|---------|
| **In my opinion** | Formal | **In my opinion**, online shopping is better. |
| **From my point of view** | Formal | **From my point of view**, physical stores are important. |
| **I believe that** | Formal | **I believe that** both options have advantages. |
| **It seems to me that** | Formal | **It seems to me that** the future is online. |
| **I consider that** | Formal | **I consider that** local shops are essential. |
| **As far as I am concerned** | Very formal | **As far as I am concerned**, we need both. |
| ~~I think~~ | Informal | Avoid in formal essays! |

### To Conclude (Para concluir)

| Connector | Example |
|-----------|---------|
| **In conclusion** | **In conclusion**, both options have benefits. |
| **To sum up** | **To sum up**, online shopping is more practical. |
| **All in all** | **All in all**, I prefer physical stores. |
| **To conclude** | **To conclude**, the best option depends on the situation. |

### To Give Reasons (Porque / Por eso)

| Connector | Example |
|-----------|---------|
| **Because of this** | You save time. **Because of this**, many people prefer it. |
| **Therefore** | It is cheaper. **Therefore**, it is more popular. |
| **As a result** | Stores are closing. **As a result**, shopping centres are empty. |
| **That is why** | It is convenient. **That is why** people choose it. |

## How to Write Each Paragraph

### Paragraph 1: Introduction

**Purpose**: Introduce the topic in a general way. Do NOT give your opinion yet.

**Useful phrases:**
- Nowadays, many people...
- In today's world...
- There are different opinions about...
- It is commonly believed that...
- Some people prefer... while others prefer...

**Example:**
> Nowadays, there are two main ways to buy products: online shopping and physical stores. Both options have advantages and disadvantages, and people have different opinions about which one is better.

---

### Paragraph 2: Opinion 1 (e.g., Advantages of Online Shopping)

**Purpose**: Present ONE side with reasons and examples.

**Structure:**
```
Topic sentence → Reason 1 → Example → Reason 2 → Example
```

**Useful phrases:**
- Firstly, one of the main advantages is...
- For example, ...
- Moreover, ...
- In addition, ...

**Example:**
> Firstly, online shopping has many advantages. Products are delivered to your home, so you do not have to leave your house. Moreover, it is usually cheaper because online stores do not have to pay rent for a physical space. In addition, you can shop at any time, which is very convenient for people with busy schedules. You also do not have to wait in a line, and you can easily return products if you are not satisfied.

---

### Paragraph 3: Opinion 2 (e.g., Advantages of Physical Stores)

**Purpose**: Present the OTHER side with reasons and examples.

**Structure:**
```
However/On the other hand → Reason 1 → Example → Reason 2 → Example
```

**Useful phrases:**
- On the other hand, ...
- However, physical stores also have benefits.
- For instance, ...
- Furthermore, ...

**Example:**
> On the other hand, physical stores also have significant advantages. For instance, you can try on clothes before buying them, which reduces the risk of choosing the wrong size. Furthermore, there are shop assistants who can help you find what you need. It is also more convenient if you buy fresh products such as fruit or vegetables. Moreover, shopping in physical stores helps the local economy and supports small businesses.

---

### Paragraph 4: Conclusion

**Purpose**: Give YOUR personal opinion and summarize.

**Useful phrases:**
- In conclusion, ...
- To sum up, ...
- In my opinion, ...
- I believe that...
- All in all, ...

**Example:**
> In conclusion, both online shopping and physical stores have their advantages. However, in my opinion, the best option depends on what you need to buy. For everyday products, I believe that online shopping is more practical and convenient. Nevertheless, for clothes or fresh food, I consider that physical stores are a better choice. All in all, I think we need both options in our lives.

## Complete Example Essay

### **Online Shopping vs Physical Stores**

Nowadays, there are two main ways to buy products: online shopping and going to a physical store. Both options have advantages and disadvantages, and people often have different opinions about which one is better.

Firstly, online shopping has several benefits. Products are delivered directly to your home, so you do not have to leave your house. Moreover, prices are usually cheaper because online stores have fewer expenses. In addition, you can shop at any time of the day, which is very convenient for people who work long hours. You also do not have to wait in a line, and if you are not satisfied, you can return the product easily. Sometimes, you even have free shipping.

On the other hand, physical stores also have significant advantages. For example, you can try on the clothes before buying them, so you know they fit correctly. Furthermore, there are shop assistants who can help you and give you advice. You can touch and see the product before you decide to buy it. In addition, if the store is in a shopping centre, you can also enjoy a coffee or have lunch while you shop. Moreover, buying in physical stores helps the local economy and is more convenient when you need fresh products.

In conclusion, both options have their benefits. However, in my opinion, online shopping is more practical for everyday products because it is faster and cheaper. Nevertheless, I believe that physical stores are essential for certain products such as clothes or food. All in all, both options complement each other and are necessary in today's world.

## Common Mistakes in Opinion Essays

| Wrong ❌ | Correct ✅ | Why? |
|----------|-----------|------|
| I **don't** like... | I **do not** like... | No contractions in formal writing |
| I **think** (x5 in essay) | I believe / In my opinion / I consider | Vary your expressions |
| **And** also... | **Moreover** / **In addition** | Use formal connectors |
| **But** it is... | **However** / **On the other hand** | Use formal connectors |
| **Because** it is cheap | **Since** it is cheaper / Due to the lower prices | Formal alternatives |
| Online shopping is **good** | Online shopping is **convenient/practical** | Use specific adjectives |
| Physical stores are **bad** | Physical stores have **some disadvantages** | Softer, more formal |

## Practice

### Exercise 1: Replace informal language

Rewrite these sentences formally:

1. "I think shopping online is cool because it's cheap."
2. "But you can't try on the clothes and that's bad."
3. "Also, there's free shipping a lot of times."

<details>
<summary>Possible Answers</summary>

1. "In my opinion, online shopping is very convenient because it is usually cheaper."
2. "However, you cannot try on the clothes, which is a significant disadvantage."
3. "Moreover, free shipping is available on many occasions."
</details>

### Exercise 2: Write Your Own Opinion Essay

Choose ONE of these topics and write a 4-paragraph essay:

1. **Living in a city vs living in the countryside**
2. **Working from home vs working in an office**
3. **Learning English online vs in a classroom**
4. **Cooking at home vs eating in restaurants**

Remember:
- 4 paragraphs (introduction, opinion 1, opinion 2, conclusion)
- Use formal connectors (moreover, however, for example...)
- No contractions
- Do not use "I think" more than once - use alternatives!

---

**Tip**: Before writing, make a table with advantages of BOTH sides (like the whiteboard!). This helps you organize your ideas before you start writing.
