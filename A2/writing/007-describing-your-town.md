# Describing Your Town or City

## Writing Task (A2)

Your favourite online magazine is asking readers to write about their town or city. Include the following:

- Where do you live? Where is it? How big is it? What is there near your town/city?
- What's your town/city like? What is there to see?
- What's the weather like in different seasons?
- What's your town/city famous for? What's the typical food?
- What's the best thing about your town/city? Do you like living there?

**Word limit**: 100-120 words.

---

## My Original Writing — Albacete

> I live in Albacete. It is in the south-east of Spain, in Castilla-La Mancha. It's not very big, but it is **~~lovely city~~**.
>
> In summer the weather is hot and dry, winter is very cold and windy. Spring and **~~autum~~** are the best.
>
> Albacete is famous for the **~~'Peria Albacete'~~**, it is **~~older~~** in Spain. People drink mojitos and eat Miguelitos, and **~~have~~** a great time. The city is famous for **~~traditional knives~~**.

### Corrections

| Mistake ❌ | Correction ✅ | Why? |
|-----------|---------------|------|
| it is **lovely city** | it is **a lovely city** | Singular countable nouns need an article (a / the) |
| **autum** | **autumn** | Spelling — silent "n" at the end |
| **Peria Albacete** | **Feria de Albacete** | Proper name in Spanish; needs "de" (of) |
| it is **older** in Spain | it is **the oldest** in Spain | Superlative form: the + adjective+est |
| and **have** a great time | and **they have** a great time | English needs a subject pronoun (unlike Spanish) |
| famous for **traditional knives** | famous for **its traditional knives** | Possessive "its" is needed before the noun |

### Corrected Version

> I live in Albacete. It is in the south-east of Spain, in Castilla-La Mancha. It's not very big, but it is **a lovely** city.
>
> In summer the weather is hot and dry, winter is very cold and windy. Spring and **autumn** are the best.
>
> Albacete is famous for the **'Feria de Albacete'**, it is **the oldest** in Spain. People drink mojitos and eat Miguelitos, and **they** have a great time. The city is famous for **its** traditional knives.

## Another Sample — Alicante

> I live in Alicante, a beautiful city on the south-east coast of Spain. It is a medium-size city with about 340,000 people. Near Alicante there are amazing beaches, mountains and small villages like Altea or Villajoyosa.
>
> Alicante is a lively and modern city. You can visit the Santa Bárbara Castle, walk along the Explanada and enjoy the old town.
>
> The weather is great almost all year. Summers are hot and sunny, winters are mild and spring is perfect for the beach.
>
> Alicante is famous for its rice dishes, like "arroz a banda", and for the "turrón" of Jijona. The best thing is the sea and the food. I love living here!

*(120 words)*

## Top Tips for This Type of Writing

### 1. Structure (4 short paragraphs)

```
┌────────────────────────────────────────┐
│  Paragraph 1 → Location + size         │
│  Paragraph 2 → What to see / do        │
│  Paragraph 3 → Weather (seasons)       │
│  Paragraph 4 → Famous for + opinion    │
└────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

### 2. Useful Vocabulary

#### Location
- in the **north / south / east / west** of Spain
- in the **south-east / north-west** of...
- on the **coast** / in the **mountains** / in the **centre**
- near... / next to... / close to...

#### Size
- a **small** village / a **medium-size** city / a **big** city
- about **50,000 / 340,000** people
- it is **not very big** / it is **quite big**

#### Description
- **lively, quiet, beautiful, historic, modern, traditional**
- **crowded, peaceful, busy, calm**

#### Weather
- Summers **are** hot / cold / mild / wet / dry
- It **rains / snows** a lot in winter
- The weather is **great / perfect / terrible** in...

#### Food & Famous Things
- It is **famous for its** + noun (castle, beaches, food...)
- People **eat / drink / cook**...
- The typical food is...

### 3. Common A2 Mistakes to Avoid

| ❌ Mistake | ✅ Correct |
|-----------|-----------|
| It is lovely city | It is **a** lovely city |
| In Spain there is many beaches | In Spain there **are** many beaches |
| Is a beautiful city | **It is** a beautiful city |
| Is famous for the food | It is famous for **its** food |
| In summer is hot | In summer **it is** hot |
| The people are very friendlys | The people are very **friendly** (no plural in adjectives) |
| I live in Alicante since 10 years | I **have lived** in Alicante **for** 10 years |

### 4. Useful Connectors (A2)

| Connector | Example |
|-----------|---------|
| **and** | It is small **and** beautiful. |
| **but** | It is small **but** lovely. |
| **also** | It is **also** very quiet. |
| **because** | I love it **because** the weather is great. |
| **so** | It is on the coast, **so** there are many beaches. |

### 5. Final Checklist Before Handing It In

- [ ] Did I write a **title**?
- [ ] Did I cover **all** the bullet points?
- [ ] Is the writing between **100-120 words**?
- [ ] Did I check **articles** (a / an / the)?
- [ ] Did I check **subject pronouns** (it, they, we)?
- [ ] Did I check **spelling** (autumn, weather, beautiful)?
- [ ] Did I use **blue or black pen**? (not pencil!)

---

**Tip**: Read your writing out loud before handing it in. If something sounds strange, it's probably wrong!
