# English Learning Project - Level A2

## Welcome to Your English Learning Journey!

This project is designed to help you learn English at an A2 (Elementary) level.

## What is A2 Level?

At A2 level, you can:
- Understand sentences and frequently used expressions
- Communicate in simple and routine tasks
- Describe your background, immediate environment, and basic needs

## Course Structure

### 1. Grammar Topics
- Present Simple and Present Continuous
- Past Simple
- Future with "going to"
- Modal verbs (can, could, should, must)
- Comparatives and Superlatives

### 2. Vocabulary Areas
- Daily routines
- Food and drinks
- Shopping
- Travel and directions
- Weather
- Family and relationships

### 3. Skills Practice
- **Reading**: Short texts and simple stories
- **Writing**: Basic emails, messages, and descriptions
- **Listening**: Everyday conversations
- **Speaking**: Common situations and dialogues
